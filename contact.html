Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start my project?

We typically can schedule projects within 1-2 weeks of estimate approval. For emergency repairs like water damage, we offer same-day or next-day service whenever possible.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes, we provide free, no-obligation estimates for all painting, drywall, and stucco projects in Billings and Yellowstone County. We'll visit your property to assess the project and provide a detailed quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, check, and all major credit cards. For larger projects, we offer payment plans with 50% due at project start and 50% upon completion.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed, bonded, and insured for your protection. We carry both liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage.

Do you provide warranties on your work?

Yes, we offer warranties on all our work. Painting projects come with a 2-year warranty, while drywall and stucco work includes a 5-year warranty against defects in workmanship.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Billings, Laurel, Shepherd, Billings Heights, West End, Downtown Billings, and all of Yellowstone County with professional painting, drywall, and stucco services.
