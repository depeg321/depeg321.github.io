About Us

Professional Stucco and Masonry Repair Specialist

With over 20 years of experience in Concrete/Stucco Restoration, we specialize in restoring damaged or remodeling of anything in masonry. Stucco, Brick, Stone, as well as matching surfaces after a new roof is installed and the new flashing is needed waterproofing and sealed.

Our team combines traditional techniques with modern materials to deliver exceptional results that stand the test of time. We take pride in our craftsmanship and attention to detail.
