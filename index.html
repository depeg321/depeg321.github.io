How to Identify and Repair Common Drywall Issues

Drywall problems are common in Montana homes due to climate changes and settling. Knowing how to identify different types of damage can save you time and money.

Common Issues:

  • Hairline cracks at corners and seams
  • Nail pops from framing movement
  • Water damage from leaks
  • Texture matching challenges

Stucco Maintenance Guide for Montana Homes

Stucco is an excellent choice for Billings homes, offering durability and energy efficiency. However, proper maintenance is essential to protect your investment.

Essential Maintenance:

  • Annual visual inspections
  • Cleaning and removing organic growth
  • Checking caulking and flashings
  • Addressing cracks promptly
