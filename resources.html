Montana-Specific Painting Tips: What Billings Homeowners Need to Know

Expert advice from an experienced painting company for Montana's unique climate challenges

Montana's climate presents unique challenges for paint and finishes. Here's what 25+ years of experience as a painting contractor and drywall contractor has taught our painting company.

Choosing Paint for Montana's Climate - Painting Company Advice

For Exteriors: Montana's extreme temperature swings, intense UV exposure, and low humidity require specific paint types for quality exterior painting services:

  • 100% Acrylic Latex: Best for Montana exteriors recommended by our painting company - flexible enough for temperature changes
  • Premium Brands: Sherwin-Williams Duration or Benjamin Moore Aura used by our painting contractor - worth the investment for painting services
  • UV Protection: Essential in Montana's high-altitude, intense sun for exterior painting
  • Mildew Resistance: Important for north-facing walls and shaded areas in our painting services

For Interiors: Low humidity affects interior paint differently in interior painting services:

  • Fast drying times (advantage in Montana for interior painting)
  • Less concern about mildew in most areas
  • Eggshell or satin finishes more durable than flat in dry climate for painting services

Common Montana Paint Problems and Solutions from Our Painting Company

Chalking: Montana's intense sun causes faster chalking (powdery surface). Solution from our painting contractor: Premium paints with UV inhibitors and repainting every 7-10 years with professional painting services.

Cracking and Peeling: Extreme temperature fluctuations cause expansion/contraction. Solution from our painting company: Proper surface prep, primer, and flexible quality paints in our painting services.

Fading: High-altitude UV is intense. Solution from our painting contractor: Lighter colors fade less visibly, or use premium fade-resistant paints in our exterior painting services.

Wood Siding Issues: Montana's dry air causes wood to split and crack. Solution from our painting company: Regular maintenance, caulking, and paint touch-ups every 2-3 years with professional painting services.

Exterior Surfaces Common in Billings - Painting Contractor Expertise

Wood Siding: Requires the most maintenance from a painting contractor - paint every 5-7 years in Montana climate with professional painting services.

Vinyl Siding: Can be painted with specific vinyl-safe paints by our painting company. Less maintenance than wood.

Stucco: Excellent for Montana handled by our stucco contractor - durable, weather-resistant. Needs repainting every 10-15 years with professional stucco services.

Metal Siding: Requires rust-inhibiting primers and metal-specific paints from our painting contractor.
